Jeep Grand Cherokee



Description The Jeep Grand Cherokee is a large, five-seat SUV and is Jeep's flagship model in India. While the previous-gen Grand Cherokee was offered with petrol and diesel powertrains, the new Grand Cherokee uses a 2.0-litre turbo-petrol engine, paired to an 8-speed automatic gearbox.
Transmission 5-speed ZF 5HP30 automatic
Engine 5.2 L M73TUB54 V8
Wheelbase 2,964 mm
